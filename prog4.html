Signature Dish: Royal Feast

Royal Feast is a magnificent blend of gourmet delicacies featuring lobster, truffle-infused sauces, and a side of perfectly grilled vegetables. It is a perfect choice for those seeking luxury and taste.

Appetizer: Spicy Shrimp Salad

Spicy Shrimp Salad is a fresh mix of shrimp, avocado, and a tangy spicy dressing, offering a delightful start to your meal. Served chilled, this dish brings a burst of flavors with every bite.

Main Course: Grilled Chicken Breast

Grilled Chicken Breast is seasoned to perfection with a blend of herbs and spices, then grilled to a crispy golden brown. It comes with a side of mashed potatoes and seasonal vegetables.

Vegetarian Option: Stuffed Bell Peppers

Stuffed Bell Peppers are filled with quinoa, black beans, and a blend of cheeses, offering a satisfying vegetarian dish full of flavor. Perfect for anyone looking for a healthy yet filling option.

Side Dish: Garlic Butter Breadsticks

Garlic Butter Breadsticks are soft and chewy, coated with a generous amount of garlic butter and sprinkled with parmesan cheese. They make a perfect side dish to complement any main course.
